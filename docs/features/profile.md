## FAQ

**Q: Can I use multiple wallets?**
A: You can connect different wallets, but one at a time.

**Q: Is my data synced across devices?**
A: Yes, all on-chain data syncs automatically.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, from Settings > Email > Change Email.

**Q: How do I enable dark mode?**
A: Settings > Appearance > Theme > Dark mode.

**Q: Where are my private keys stored?**
A: StackSave never stores private keys. They remain in your wallet.

**Q: Can I export my data?**
A: Yes, from Profile > Export Your Data.
