## FAQ

**Q: How long does a withdrawal take?**
A: Usually 30 seconds to 5 minutes, depending on gas price and network.

**Q: Can I cancel a withdrawal?**
A: Once submitted to blockchain, it cannot be cancelled. Only reject in wallet before signing.

**Q: Is there a minimum withdrawal?**
A: No platform minimum, but consider gas fees for small amounts.

**Q: Do I pay fees to StackSave?**
A: No! You only pay blockchain gas fees.

**Q: What if I withdraw to wrong address?**
A: Blockchain transactions are irreversible. Always verify address!

**Q: Can I withdraw while offline?**
A: No, you need internet connection to submit blockchain transaction.

**Q: Will I lose my goal if I withdraw all funds?**
A: No, goal remains. You can add funds again anytime.

**Q: Can I undo a withdrawal?**
A: No, blockchain transactions are final. You can deposit again though.
