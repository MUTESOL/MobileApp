## Understanding Your Goal Card

Each goal displays:

### Visual Elements
- **Progress Bar**: Visual representation of progress
- **Percentage**: Numerical progress (0-100%)
- **Icon**: Category icon you selected
- **Flame Icon**: Savings streak indicator

### Information Displayed
- **Goal Name**: Top of the card
- **Amount Saved**: Current total saved
- **Target Amount**: Your goal target
- **Progress**: "450 / 2,500 USDC" format
- **Time Remaining**: Days until deadline
- **Last Activity**: When you last deposited

### Action Buttons
- **Add Savings**: Make a deposit
- **View Details**: See full goal information
- **Edit Goal**: Modify details
- **Withdraw**: Remove funds (appears when balance > 0)

## Making Your First Deposit

Now that your goal exists, let's fund it!

### Quick Deposit

1. Tap **"Add Savings"** on your goal card
2. Enter deposit amount
3. Review transaction:
   - Amount to deposit
   - Current gas fees
   - Total cost
4. Tap **"Confirm"**
5. Approve in wallet app
6. Wait for confirmation (~30-60 seconds)
7. Watch your progress bar grow! ðŸŽŠ

### Detailed Steps

#### 1. Enter Amount
- Type the amount to deposit
- Use number pad for precise entry
- Or tap preset amounts (if available)
- See running total as you type

#### 2. Review Transaction
Before confirming, check:
- **Deposit Amount**: What goes to your goal
- **Gas Fee**: Network transaction cost
- **Total Cost**: Deposit + gas (deducted from wallet)
- **New Balance**: Your goal balance after deposit
- **New Progress**: Updated percentage

#### 3. Wallet Approval
Your wallet shows:
- Transaction details
- Smart contract interaction (if applicable)
- Gas settings (can often be adjusted)
- Total cost

Review carefully, then approve.

#### 4. Transaction Pending
- StackSave shows "Pending..." status
- Don't close the app (recommended)
- Usually takes 30-60 seconds
- Can view on blockchain explorer

#### 5. Success! âœ…
When confirmed:
- Success message appears
- Progress bar animates to new level
- Amount updates
- Notification may appear
- Transaction added to history

## Goal Management

### Viewing Goal Details

Tap on any goal to see:
- Full statistics
- Transaction history
- Progress over time
- Edit options

### Editing a Goal

To modify goal details:

1. Tap goal card
2. Tap **"Edit"** or gear icon
3. Change any field:
   - Name
   - Target amount (up or down)
   - Deadline
   - Category
4. Tap **"Save Changes"**

**Note**: Can't edit while transaction is pending.

### Tracking Progress

Monitor your goal through:
- **Progress Percentage**: Visual completion
- **Amount Chart**: Savings over time
- **Velocity**: Rate of progress
- **Projection**: Estimated completion date

### Deleting a Goal

To remove a goal:

1. Open goal details
2. Scroll to bottom
3. Tap **"Delete Goal"**
4. Confirm deletion

**Important**:
- Can only delete if balance is 0
- Must withdraw funds first if any exist
- Deletion is permanent

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I have multiple goals?**
A: Yes! Create as many as you need.

**Q: Can I change the target amount later?**
A: Yes, goals are flexible. Edit anytime.

**Q: What if I can't meet my deadline?**
A: No problem! Extend it or keep saving at your own pace.

**Q: Can I deposit to multiple goals at once?**
A: Not in one transaction, but you can quickly switch between goals.

**Q: Is there a minimum deposit?**
A: Only what's needed to cover gas fees. Even small amounts work!

**Q: Can I auto-deposit regularly?**
A: Not yet, but it's coming! For now, set reminders.
